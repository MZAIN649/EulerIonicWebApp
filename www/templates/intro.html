Inputs
Inputs tab is used to set values of initial conditions. Press 'Set Inputs' button after changing inputs.
Simulation
Simulation tab is used for displaying simulation, you can change orientation by sliding over the body. Also value of Euler Parameters, Euler Angles and Angular Velocity at specific time can be noted by pausing the simulation.
Graphs
Graphs tab will display graphs of Euler Parameters, Euler Angles and Angular Velocity for complete simulation.
